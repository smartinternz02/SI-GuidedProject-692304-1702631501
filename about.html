Medical Charges Analysis

In the Credit Card Customer Segmentation project, the objective is to utilize Principal Component Analysis (PCA) and K-means clustering to analyze and categorize credit card customers based on their spending behaviors and financial characteristics. The project involves preprocessing and scaling the credit card transaction data, reducing dimensionality using PCA to capture the most significant features, and then applying K-means clustering to group customers into distinct segments. These segments may represent different types of credit card users, such as low spenders, high spenders, responsible users, or occasional users. By understanding these segments, financial institutions can tailor their marketing strategies, product offerings, and customer services to better meet the unique needs and preferences of each segment,ultimately improving customer satisfaction and loyalty while optimizing business outcomes.
